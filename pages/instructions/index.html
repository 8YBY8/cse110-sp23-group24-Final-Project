Instructions

Get your future read with tarot cards!
Follow the instructions below to get a reading.
  • Pick 3 cards from the given selection of cards. There is no wrong card to pick!
  • After you click each card, information about your past, present, or future will be shown.
  • Take what resonates with you, and refresh the Homepage again to get a new reading.
    • Helpful tips
  • The first card you choose will give you a reading on your past. This may represent something from your past that will affect your future and is leading to your current state of mind.
  • The second card you choose will give you a reading on your present. This may represent your current state of mind or decisions you may need to make.
  • The third card you choose will give you a reading on your future. This may represent what will occur once you make a decision based on your present or what the future will hold for you.
  • There is no right or wrong answer for choosing cards. Choose a card that you feel like you are drawn to, or just choose randomly!
    • Want to get your future read in a different way?

    Head to our Homepage and select our 8 ball option to use an 8 ball to read your future!
